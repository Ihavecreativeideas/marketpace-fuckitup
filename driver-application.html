Background Check Requirements

Uber Eats-Level Standards Required

All drivers must pass the same background check standards used by Uber Eats. You are responsible for obtaining and providing your own background check from an approved provider.

Criminal History Check

  • No violent crimes in the past 7 years
  • No theft or property crimes in past 7 years
  • No drug-related felonies in past 7 years
  • No sexual offenses (any timeframe)
  • No DUI/DWI in past 7 years

Driving Record Check

  • Valid driver's license for 1+ years
  • No reckless driving in past 3 years
  • No more than 3 moving violations in 3 years
  • No license suspension in past 3 years
  • Current auto insurance meeting state minimums
