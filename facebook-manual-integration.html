📋 Facebook Manual Integration

🚀 Direct Facebook Shop Connection

Since Facebook App OAuth is encountering configuration issues, we'll set up a manual integration that connects your Facebook Shop directly to MarketPace delivery services.

📚 Integration Steps

1

Prepare Your Facebook Shop Information

Gather the following information from your Facebook Shop:

  • Facebook Page Name and URL
  • Shop/Business Manager ID
  • Product Catalog Information
  • Administrator Access
2

Configure MarketPace Delivery

Set up delivery options and radius for your Facebook Shop products:

  • Delivery radius (5-50 miles)
  • Delivery fees and pricing
  • Operating hours and availability
  • Special delivery instructions
3

Add Delivery Links to Facebook

We'll generate custom delivery links that you can add to your Facebook Shop posts:

  • Product-specific delivery links
  • "Deliver Now" button integration
  • Custom landing pages for Facebook customers
  • Order tracking and management
