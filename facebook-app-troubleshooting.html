üìä Error Analysis: "Sorry, something went wrong"

This Facebook error indicates fundamental Facebook App configuration issues beyond redirect URIs. Common causes include:

  • App is in Development Mode with restricted access
  • App Review required for business permissions
  • Business Verification needed for commerce features
  • Domain verification required
  • App permissions not properly configured

üéØ Critical Configuration Checklist

1

App Review Status

Most Critical: Check if your app needs Facebook App Review for business permissions.

Required Permissions: - pages_show_list (May require review) - catalog_management (Requires business verification) - business_management (Requires review) - commerce_account_read_orders (Requires review)
Check App Review Status
2

Development Mode vs Live Mode

If app is in Development Mode, only app admins/developers can use OAuth. You need to either:

  • Add test users in Development Settings
  • Switch app to Live Mode (requires App Review)
Check App Mode Add Test Users
3

Business Verification

Commerce permissions require Business Verification. Check your Business Manager:

Business Verification
4

Domain Verification

Add and verify marketpace.shop domain in Facebook App settings:

Domains to add: - marketpace.shop - www.marketpace.shop
