Methods to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Self-Service Account Deletion

  1. Log into your MarketPace account
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your identity
  5. Select reason for deletion (optional)
  6. Click "Permanently Delete Account"

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@marketpace.shop with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Account email address
  • Phone number (for verification)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Method 3: Facebook App Data Deletion

If you connected via Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings ‚Üí Apps and Websites
  2. Find "MarketPace" in your connected apps
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access
  4. Visit this page to delete MarketPace account data
