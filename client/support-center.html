Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start selling on MarketPace?

+

To start selling on MarketPace: 1) Create your account through Sign Up/Login, 2) Go to the Community page and click "Post" to create a sale listing, 3) Add photos, description, and price, 4) Choose delivery options (MarketPace delivery or self pickup), 5) Your item will appear in the community feed for local buyers to discover.

How does MarketPace delivery work?

+

MarketPace delivery connects buyers and sellers with local drivers. When you purchase an item, you can choose MarketPace delivery. Our drivers pick up from the seller and deliver to your address. Delivery fees are split 50/50 between buyer and seller. Track your delivery in real-time through the Delivery dashboard.

What is MarketPace Pro?

+

MarketPace Pro offers enhanced business features including: product promotion tools, business dashboard, revenue tracking, platform integrations (Facebook, Shopify), and priority support. Pro members can promote products 50-70% cheaper than Facebook ads with no Apple fees.

How do I become a driver?

+

To become a MarketPace driver: 1) Complete the driver application with required documents (Driver's License, Insurance, Background Check), 2) Wait for admin approval, 3) Once approved, access delivery routes through the Driver Dashboard, 4) Choose time slots and routes that work for your schedule. Earn $4 per pickup + $2 per dropoff + $0.50/mile + 100% tips.

Payment and fees information

+

MarketPace uses Stripe for secure payments. Platform takes 5% commission on sales. Delivery fees are split 50/50 between buyer and seller. Drivers receive 100% of customer tips. Pro member promotions have no additional fees beyond the promotion cost. All payments are processed securely with industry-standard encryption.

Account and privacy settings

+

Access your account settings through the Menu > Account Settings. You can update personal information, payment methods, delivery addresses, and privacy preferences. MarketPace NEVER sells user data to outside parties. All data stays within the platform for community commerce purposes only.

The Hub - Entertainment features

+

The Hub is MarketPace's entertainment network for musicians, DJs, comedians, and performers. Features include: 100% commission-free ticket sharing, gear marketplace, professional profiles, event promotion, and social networking. All entertainment professionals can promote events and connect with local audiences at no cost.
