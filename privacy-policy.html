MarketPace Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile information.

Business Information: For business accounts, we collect business name, address, contact details, and business category.

Facebook Integration: When you connect Facebook, we access your public profile and business page information with your permission.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our platform, including listings viewed, searches performed, and platform interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our marketplace services
  • Facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Send important account and transaction notifications
  • Connect your MarketPace listings with your Facebook business page
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Facebook Integration

When you connect your Facebook account:

  • We access your public profile information (name, profile picture)
  • We can post to your connected Facebook business pages
  • We respond to marketplace inquiries via Facebook Messenger
  • We do not post to your personal Facebook timeline without permission
  • You can disconnect Facebook integration at any time

4. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

We only share information:

  • With other users as necessary for transactions (contact info for delivery)
  • With service providers who help operate our platform
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Facebook when you use Facebook integration features

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal information

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disconnect Facebook integration

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain some information for legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution.

8. Children's Privacy

MarketPace is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.
