MarketPace Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We're committed to protecting your personal information.

Our Privacy Promise: MarketPace NEVER sells your personal data to outside parties. We believe your information belongs to you, and we're committed to keeping it safe and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile picture, phone number
  • Business Information: Business name, category, operating hours, location
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Communications: Messages, customer support inquiries, feedback
  • Content: Product listings, reviews, photos, posts you create

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Location Data: General location for local marketplace features (with your permission)
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for functionality, performance, and security

Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: Basic profile information when you connect Facebook, Google, or other accounts
  • Payment Processors: Transaction verification from Stripe
  • Business Partners: Information from integrated platforms (with your consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

Platform Operations

  • Provide and maintain MarketPace services
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Manage user accounts and profiles
  • Provide customer support

Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Develop new products and services
  • Conduct research and analytics

Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Verify user identity and prevent fake accounts
  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

Communications

  • Send important account and service updates
  • Notify you about new features and improvements
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Zero External Data Sales: We NEVER sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

When We Share Information

  • With Other Users: Your public profile, listings, and reviews are visible to other MarketPace users
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our platform (payment processing, hosting, customer support)
  • Business Transfers: If MarketPace is acquired, your information may be transferred to the new owner
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing with specific third parties

Third-Party Integrations

When you connect external services (Facebook, Shopify, etc.), we only share information necessary for the integration to function. You can disconnect these services at any time.

4. Data Security

Enterprise-Grade Security: We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data.

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Database Security: Row Level Security ensuring users only access their own data
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments and penetration testing
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

Physical and Environmental

  • Secure data centers with biometric access controls
  • Environmental monitoring and fire suppression systems
  • Backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Regular security training for all employees

5. Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

Marketing Communications

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Opt out of promotional SMS messages
  • Adjust notification preferences in your account settings

Account Deletion

You can delete your MarketPace account at any time. We will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days, except for information we're required to retain for legal compliance.

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Account Information Until account deletion Provide service

7. International Data Transfers

MarketPace is based in the United States. Your information is primarily stored and processed in the United States, which may have different privacy laws than your country.

Safeguards for International Transfers

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • Adequate security measures for all data transfers
  • Regular compliance reviews and audits
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-company transfers

8. Children's Privacy

MarketPace is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Teen Users (13-17)

  • Parental consent required for account creation
  • Limited data collection and sharing
  • Enhanced privacy protections
  • Restricted access to certain features

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you via email or platform notification
  • Provide a summary of key changes
  • Give you time to review before changes take effect

Continued use of MarketPace after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. State-Specific Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Virginia Residents (VCDPA)

  • Right to access, correct, and delete personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to opt-out of targeted advertising
  • Right to opt-out of profiling for decisions with legal effects

Colorado Residents (CPA)

  • Right to access and delete personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to opt-out of targeted advertising and profiling
