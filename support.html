Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? +
You can create an account by clicking "Sign Up" and choosing from Facebook, Google, or email registration. Fill out your profile information and you're ready to start using MarketPace!
How does the rental system work? +
Browse rental items in your area, contact the owner, and arrange pickup or delivery. Payment is handled securely through the platform. Remember to check item condition before finalizing the rental.
What are the delivery options? +
We offer MarketPace delivery through our driver network, self-pickup, and scheduled delivery options. Delivery fees are split 50/50 between buyer and seller, with tips going 100% to drivers.
How do I become a driver? +
Apply through our driver application with your license, insurance, and background check. Once approved, you can accept delivery routes and earn $4 per pickup, $2 per dropoff, plus $0.50 per mile and 100% of tips.
Is my personal information safe? +
Yes! MarketPace NEVER sells your data to outside parties. We use enterprise-grade security with anti-bot protection, data privacy enforcement, and GDPR compliance to keep your information secure.
How do payments work? +
All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Sellers receive payment after successful delivery, minus platform fees. Delivery costs are split between buyer and seller automatically.
